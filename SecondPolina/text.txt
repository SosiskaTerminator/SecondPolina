In a forest where mists kiss the edge of the dawn,
Lived a dreamy young hedgehog by name of Leaf.
He believed that beyond the far mountains of blue,
Grew a tiny bright ember to comfort and shine.


When a moonless night settled soft on the wood,
He walked down a trail where the dewdrops would gleam.
A golden firefly winked as he quietly passed,
And the wind whispered gently, Go on, trust your dream.
